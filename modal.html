"Rough day? Cough it out."

A C and T user shares his story

I work hard every day. Sometimes things get so tough that I start wondering what the point is. Then I remember that at 5 o'clock, I get to clock out, go to the Chester County Library's Exton branch, and cough my lungs out until closing time.

I used to be like everybody else. Keeping it in. Then, one day, riding the train to Doylestown, I sat next to a man who simply dominated the quiet car with his great and terrible expectorations. The car shook with each of his mammoth heaves. I knew then that there was no looking back.

Since then, I've been amazed at the situations I've been able to ruin by barking as wetly as I can. Coughin and Talkin has really emboldened me to explore cough culture and reach out to others like me, all of whom are the greatest people around.
